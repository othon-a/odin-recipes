Breakfast Tacos

Description

Delicious and hearty, breakfast tacos may truly be the breakfast of champions.

Ingredients

  • Tortillas
  • Eggs
  • Bacon
  • Refried Beans
  • Jack Cheese

Steps

  • Heat tortillas and set aside in tortilla warmer
  • Heat pacon in pan until cooked
  • Using same pan, scramble eggs with jack cheese until cooked
  • Add refried beans, bacon, and scrambled eggs with cheese to tortillas
  • Top with your favorite hot sauce and serve
