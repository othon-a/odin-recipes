Carbonara

Description

Lovingly described as Italian mac and cheese, carbonara is an easy-to-create dish that is sure to bring comfort to any palate.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Guanciale
  • Egg Yolk
  • Hard Cheese
  • Black Pepper

Steps

  • Boil spaghetti for 10 minutes
  • Strain spaghetti, setting aside a little pasta water
  • In pan, cook guanciale
  • Add cheese and pasta water to pan until melted and creamy
  • Reduce heat and add spaghetti to pan
  • Add egg yolks to spaghetti and mix thoroughly
  • Serve and top with black pepper
